# **Welcome to FizzCraft 2**

A world of exciting opportunities from space to a world of bees, and with version 1.20.1 we also get the introduction of Archeology. So have fun and enjoy FizzCraft 2.

FizzCraft and FizzCraft 2 are not completely compatible as they use different world generation, **Use at your own risk**.
